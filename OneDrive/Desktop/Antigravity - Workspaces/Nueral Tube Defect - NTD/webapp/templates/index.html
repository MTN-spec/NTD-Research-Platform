Methodology
ML pipeline for integrated multi-omics NTD prediction
1
Data Acquisition
Genomic variants from 1000 Genomes Project & gene expression from GTEx Consortium
2
Preprocessing
Variant filtering, MAF/HWE QC, TPM normalisation, and additive genotype encoding
3
Feature Engineering
Pathway risk scores, gene-gene interactions, and PCA dimensionality reduction
4
Model Training
Random Forest, XGBoost, and Deep Neural Network with 5-fold stratified CV
5
Evaluation
AUC-ROC, F1-Score, precision/recall analysis on held-out test set
6
Interpretation
Feature importance ranking and SHAP analysis for biomarker discovery
